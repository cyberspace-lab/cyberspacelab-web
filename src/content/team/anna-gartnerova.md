I am in my third year of the Humanities and Social Sciences programme at the Faculty of Humanities at Charles University. I have always been interested in psychology, particularly cognitive psychology and neuropsychology. Although my primary interest lies in these areas, I see CyberspaceLab as an opportunity to broaden my perspective and explore how psychological processes can be studied in connection with modern technologies.
